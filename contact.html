FAQ

Who can travel to North Korea?

Anyone, excluding Americans, South Koreans and other minor nationalities. You do require a tour approved by a governement-run agency however.

How can I travel to North Korea?

Currently, Travel is heavily restricted and is mostly closed to tourists, but Russian tourists are more welcome.

What is Pyongyang known for?

Pyongyang is known for its distinctive modernist architecture, which blends Soviet and traditional Korean styles and for being the capital city of North Korea.
